Bookshelf

I enjoy reading on my free time. This is a list of books I've read or made a good amount of progress in reading. This is by no means a comprehensive list and mostly includes the most recent books I've read. I also start more books than I finish meaning some of those books might not make it into this list. I think thinking you have to finish reading all of the books you start is an example of a sunk cost fallacy. This way I can get through more books without getting stuck on a book for too long.

I'll try to update this list every so often.

Although I prefer reading on physical books, these days I mostly read in a Kindle Paperwhite. Sometimes I'll listen to audiobooks with Audible and will have them sync with the kindle version of the book. I also, try to keep track of all the books I am currently reading and those that I am interested in reading in the future on goodreads.

Here's the list. Recommended titles are in bold modeled after Aaron Swartz's Review of Books and Tommy Collison's books list. Books are classified as non-technical or non-technical books, other than that, similar to Patrick Collison's Bookshelf these books are not in any particular order.

I love talking about books. Feel free to email me books you recommend reading or message me onTwitter.
