Alejandro García Salas

Hi! I am a peruvian software engineer, designer, and artist living in San Francisco, California. Currently, I work as a founding engineer at Sail, building a new way to experience the web together.

Previously, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Airbnb building and prototyping new search experiences. In the past, I studied computer science and cognitive science at UC Berkeley with a focus on human-computer-interaction. In college, I interned at Airbnb and Pinterest working on design systems and web infrastructure. In high school I interned at Laboratoria, a web dev bootcamp, and Ayu, a small digital agency based out of Lima, Perú. I first got into tech by programming and designing my own video games and I haven't stopped making things ever since. You can find my résumé here.

I strive to live a life of adventure and service while building the future I want to see. At the moment, I am broadly interested in globalization, newly industrialized countries, decentralized apps, developer/design/creative tools, and the pluriverse.

Two of my biggest joys are creating and understanding. In addition to programming, I like to oil paint, visit museums, watch documentaries, travel and learn new languages. I am also a huge fan of tea and long walks.

I am an optimist at heart, I live true to myself and I live in the present. I aim for Balance and I follow my heart and intuition. I exist first and foremost to love.
